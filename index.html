Solar Education

Learn: Net-Metering & Government Subsidy

Understand how you earn credits from excess solar power, and how to claim your government subsidy.

☀️ Daytime: Your solar panels power your home directly. Excess electricity flows to the grid — your meter runs backwards! You earn credits for every unit exported.

✅ PM Surya Ghar Eligibility

  • Indian citizen with residential electricity connection
  • Roof-top installation only (no ground-mounted)
  • Grid-connected (on-grid) system — no off-grid
  • Roof/property ownership or owner's written consent
  • Valid consumer number with DISCOM (MPPKVVCL / MPMKVVCL)
  • Aadhaar-linked bank account for direct subsidy transfer
  • Net metering approved by DISCOM before installation

📄 Documents Checklist

  • Latest 3 months' electricity bills (with Consumer ID)
  • Aadhaar card (applicant + property owner)
  • PAN card
  • Bank passbook / cancelled cheque
  • Property ownership proof (sale deed / khatauni)
  • Geo-tagged rooftop photographs (min 4 angles)
  • Signed application form (Suryadoot will help you fill)
